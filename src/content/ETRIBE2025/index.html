About Spencer Lindsay


Technical Art Director • Interactive Experience Designer • Creative Technology Consultant


Spencer Lindsay is a veteran Technical Art Director with over 35 years of experience developing immersive, real-time 3D content and interactive installations. From early contributions at Atari Games and Rockstar San Diego to pioneering work in AR/VR at Magic Leap, Spencer blends deep technical expertise with a strong creative vision.

As founder of ETRIBE, Spencer leads the design and deployment of interactive experiences for museums, aquariums, public spaces, and corporate clients. Projects span from gaze-based storytelling exhibits and real-time projection mapping to volumetric capture and advanced photogrammetry workflows.

With a sharp focus on user experience, Spencer’s practice integrates:

  • TouchDesigner, Unity, Blender, and custom shaders for real-time environments
  • Projection mapping with MadMapper + MiniMad
  • Web-based 3D using PlayCanvas and Three.js
  • Sensor integration for motion-reactive installations
  • Photogrammetry + Gaussian splatting for environmental scanning
  • Procedural animation and generative design pipelines
  • Consulting for creative technology, R&D strategy, and rapid prototyping



Known for bridging design and engineering, Spencer brings clarity to complex projects, aligning technical pipelines with artistic goals — from early concept through delivery.
